SUMMARY

Today, obesity affects about 69% of Americans, which means that it is more than one-third of the population in the United States. Obesity is when a person has excessive amounts of adipose tissue. Adipose tissue is the body fat found all over the body. This can eventually lead up to type two diabetes or even death. The negative impact of obesity has been worsening over the years but so far there hasn't been a sign of relief. It is difficult for people with obesity to lose weight based just on lifestyle changes. Wearable devices are a method that can help control, reduce, or even get rid of obesity.
