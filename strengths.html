MY THREE STRENGTHS

FUTURE THINKER

This means my mind loves to think and dream about the future. I think about what is possible, and not what is impossible. I like to imagine the life I'll have. What I will be able to do that I can't do now. I like to think about the important things in life, what they mean, and how they are connected to each other. I spend time thinking about ideas, not just facts.

HOW I CAN USE THIS STRENGTH
    PLAN FUTURE EVENTS
    CLEARLY THINK ABOUT WHAT I WANT TO BE LATER ON

COMPETING

I see many things in life as a game, and I feel great joy when I win. I also truly hate to lose because I'm always striving for first place. Every day I compete. Winning first place is my goal in any contest that I feel is worth my time. If there is no one to compete against, I compete against myself to do more or accomplish a goal faster or better. Because I hate to lose, I might get angry or upset. It does not matter if others think the contest is important. If I'm doing it, I want to be number one.

HOW I CAN USE THIS STRENGTH
    PUSH MYSELF TO DO THE BEST I CAN
    CHALLENGE MYSELF AND DISCOVER NEW THINGS

CONFIDENCE

This means I believe in myself and in what I can do. This helps me take on challenges to feel sure I can succeed. I am able to do things that others may not want to try. I don't have to be like everybody else, and I don't have to follow the rest of the crowd.

HOW I CAN USE THIS STRENGTH
    MOTIVATE MYSELF
    OVERCOME MY FEARS
